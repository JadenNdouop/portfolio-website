About Me

Hey there 👋

I'm Jaden — a psychologist, data scientist, and founder who sits at the intersection of human behavior and technology. My path has been anything but linear, and I think that's the point.

After studying psychology at UMass Lowell and data science at Boston University, I realized the most powerful problems to solve aren't technical ones — they're human ones. That insight led me to found StudyWiser, an EdTech company built to help students learn smarter.

I believe understanding how people think is the ultimate superpower. Everything I build starts there.
